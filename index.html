A Little About Me

My name is Joseph Soria, and welcome to my portfolio! Who am I? I am a University of Houston graduate, earning my Bachelor's of Science in Mechanical Engineering. I have worked as a professional in the Oil and Gas Industry since 2013, and have experience in Quality and Supply Chain Management.

In 2020, I embarked on my journey to explore the world of Fullstack Web Development. Joining the bootcamp force, I undertook a Trilogy program that partnered with Rice University. Six months later, I graduated the bootcamp with flying colors, making great friends and building an impressive Github portfolio in the process.

Ever since, I have been creating small projects to hone my skills. My focus was in building applications using React.js as my frontend library. I have built applications as a Fullstack Developer, coding everything from the frontend to the backend.

Now I'm preparing for the next big move in my life. I am open to Frontend Development opportunities, and I hope that my portfolio speaks to my potential in the industry!
